Privacy Policy for AirHorizon

Last updated: May 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Valerii Nikolaev ("we," "us," "our," or the "developer") handles information in connection with the AirHorizon iOS and iPadOS application (the "App"). AirHorizon is designed to process flight display data on your device. We do not require an account, sell personal information, or use third-party advertising or third-party analytics SDKs in the App.

Information Processed by the App

AirHorizon may process the following information on your device to provide its features:

  • device motion and orientation data, including accelerometer, gyroscope, and device-motion output;
  • heading or compass data when heading features are used;
  • location-derived data, such as course, speed, altitude, and related accuracy values, when flight data or heading features require it and permission is granted;
  • barometric or pressure-sensor data where supported by the device and selected by the user;
  • local-network telemetry received from a simulator or other user-configured source, which may include pitch, roll, heading, speed, altitude, and flight-director cues;
  • App settings and preferences, such as calibration, display style, units, trims, night-vision settings, and premium feature preferences;
  • purchase and entitlement status provided by Apple StoreKit.

This information is used to operate App features and is not sent to the developer's servers.

Location, Motion, Barometer, and Local Network Permissions

AirHorizon requests permissions only when needed for features that use those capabilities. You can allow or deny permissions in iOS Settings.

Location and heading services may be used for heading, speed, altitude, course, and related flight-data displays. Motion and barometric data may be used for attitude, calibration, and altitude-related displays. Local Network access may be used to receive simulator telemetry from a source configured by you on your local network.

If a permission is denied or unavailable, the related feature may be limited, unavailable, or less accurate.

External Telemetry and Simulators

When you enable external telemetry features, AirHorizon may listen for data on the local network from a simulator or other user-configured source. The App processes that telemetry locally for display and guidance features. AirHorizon does not transmit that telemetry to the developer.

Your simulator, router, operating system, local network, or other third-party tools may process network data under their own terms and privacy practices. Those systems are outside the developer's control.

Purchases and Subscriptions

Purchases, subscriptions, trials, renewals, cancellations, refunds, and payment details are handled by Apple through the App Store and StoreKit. AirHorizon receives entitlement information from Apple so it can unlock or lock paid features. We do not receive your full payment card details.

Data Storage and Retention

AirHorizon stores App settings and preferences locally on your device. These settings may be included in device backups depending on your Apple and iOS backup settings. Deleting the App generally removes locally stored App data from the device, subject to Apple backup and restore behavior.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. We do not use third-party advertising networks. We do not intentionally share your sensor, location, barometer, or external telemetry data with the developer or third parties.

Information may be processed by Apple or other third-party platform providers when you use operating-system services, App Store purchases, Safari or web links, GitHub Pages-hosted legal pages, device backups, or other platform features. Those services are governed by their own terms and privacy policies.

Links to External Websites

The App may open external websites, including legal pages, support links, App Store subscription management, or third-party documentation. We are not responsible for the content, security, or privacy practices of external websites.

Children's Privacy

AirHorizon is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions may be posted in the App, on our website, or otherwise made available. Your continued use of the App after an updated Privacy Policy is made available means you acknowledge the updated policy.
